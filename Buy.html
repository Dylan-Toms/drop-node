Compatible Boards and
Drives

The ODROID C4 is the ONLY supported computer at this time.
While the Raspi 4 will fit this design, it is not recommended.
Mechanical spinning hard drives require too much power for the pi to handle.
The ODROID can output more power and is similarly priced to the Raspi 4.
Even using 2x SSDs with a Raspi 4 can result in malfunction if the pi is under significant load.

The goal of this project is to attach 2 disks to a computer with a small form factor. Sadly, the
Raspberry Pi is not the best board for this task.

Full Parts List

  • ODROID C4 (or another compatible board)
  • Power Supply
  • 2x Hard drives or SSDs
  • 2x USB3.0 to SATA adapter
  • 2x USB3.0 180 degree connector
  • 2x 40mm 5v fans
  • 3D printed frame
  • minimum 8x M3 6mm screws
